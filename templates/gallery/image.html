“Cosmic Cliffs” in the Carina Nebula

July 12th, 2022

What looks a lot like rugged mountains in a night moonlit is actually the edge of a young, nearby star-forming region NGC 3324 in the Carina Nebula. Captured in infrared light by Near-Infrared Camera (NIRCam) on NASA's James Webb Space Telescope, this image reveals previously obscured areas of star birth.
